The Concept

A new ritual for
the joy of guessing

Gamif turns shopping into a film you star in. Pick three items — a main surprise, a sweet companion, a small token — pay a box price that's always lower than retail, and let the wheel decide the rest.

Behind every order is a server-side spin that may grant free delivery, a steeper discount, a bonus gift, an upgrade, or a mystery item drawn from this season's stock. The moment of the unboxing belongs to you. The story you tell with it is ours together.

The Story
"We built Gamif because shopping should feel like the first scene of a film — three picks, a spin of fate, and a moment worth filming."
Gamif Studio
Tbilisi  ·  Established 2024

Gamif is a mystery-box e-commerce platform built for the Georgian market and the global feed. Every order begins with three picks across our curated catalogue, completes with a server-validated lucky-wheel spin, and ends in an unboxing moment crafted to be shared.

Behind the scenes, products sync nightly from our backoffice, stock is reconciled live, and every box price is verified to beat retail before checkout. Payments clear through Stripe in Georgian lari. What you see is what arrives — plus, almost always, something extra.

This is not a catalogue. It is a ritual. And it begins the moment you press play.
